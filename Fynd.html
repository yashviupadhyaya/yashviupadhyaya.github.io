An application for finding Rooms and roommates

Tags : Design Thinking, Prototyping - December,2018

Overview

This project was developed as a part of the UXD winter school, hosted at IIIT Hyderabad by Dr. Aman Parnami and Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru "PK". We were teamed up in a group of 3 and asked to research on one problem and come up with a solution. Having redefined the user flow and redesigned the interaction elements I could create an entirely new application with a reduced number of click and task time

Problem Brief

Every person who moves to a new city searches for a place to live in. With the increasing cost of living and lifestyle standards, most of them choose to share their rents with a roommate. However, a combination of the preferred room and a roommate is a problem unsolved. Hence, I initiated this design thinking project.

User Research

1. Understanding the problem and the target users:

With research, I wanted to study the difficulties faced by the people that visit a new place to pursue higher studies, job or any other reason. I also wanted to understand their choice of living, preferences and the methods that they currently follow to find a place of accommodation and roommates if any.

2. Analysing the data:

To understand and classify major problems faced by the users I made an affinity diagram from the collected data. Clustering the findings helped me define the major requirements of the users. We used a collaborative tool - Miro to create affinity diagrams.

  1. User Prefers - Age, Gender, Language, Culture, Hobbies, Educational and Professional Background
  2. User does - Online search, Post on social media, Applications, Printing and distributing pamphlets
  3. User goals - Find prefered rooms, save money, share room and rent, share food
