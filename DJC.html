Redesign Casestudy

Tags : Design, Development - 17 june 2019

Problem Statement

This project was created to bridge the communication gap between students and teachers, enhancing the flow of information between them. Currently, our college website has all the details regarding the admissions and other procedures which majorly targets the prospective students and passed out alumni. However, there is no provision made for the current students and the faculty for sharing information or building network. Additionally, there does not exist any addon for the same. For this, I planned to introduce an additional feature which would facilitate the communication between students and teachers.

Knowing the problem

As a student of the college, I knew what all facilities does the college website lack. Additionally, I interviewed a set of students from the college with a set of questions as follows:

  • Where do you save and maintain your profile including grades, attendance, projects, etc?
  • How do you let other students of the college know about you and your work?
  • How do you find a teacher or alumni for a particular doubt or interest?
  • What are the other features that you wish to add to the currently existing college website?

From the responses of the students, I could identify various shortcomings of the current DJConnect--College-communication-Website:

Findings

With the data collected from the survey and the observations of task analysis, I could create an empathy map which helped me define the target users, the purpose of use of the application and the challenges faced.

Identified Problems:

  • No place to keep track of attendance, grades
  • Network building not possible and exists no information of the teachers, alumni and current students of the college.
  • Cannot access or issue library books online.

Solution:

  • Creating and maintaining a Profile that others can search and view on the portal
  • Connecting to teachers and alumni from the portal by searching their profile.
  • Issuing Books from the library online
