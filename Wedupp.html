Wedupp

Overview

Wedupp is a B2B and B2C commercial platform which provides services to the wedding planners in the form of an information management portal and a team collaborative platform. Additionally, the company provides a mobile application which is to be used by the poeple attending the wedding. This application not only enables wedding planners to collect information of the attendees in the weddings but also to manage the guest list by send invitations, collecting RSVP, etc.

My Role

I work at Wedupp Pvt Ltd. as a UI/UX design Lead. My responsibility was to revamp the website's and mobile applicatoin's user interface and experience. I worked closely with the development team and the founders to develop an entirely new information architecture, determine the new colour scheme of the website, design an optimized information system for the wedding planners and finally prototyping the interactions with improved usability.

The modules that are worked on are as follows:

  1. Wedupp mobile application- View
  2. Wedupp web portal for Wedding Planners
  3. Wedupp Team collaboration platform
